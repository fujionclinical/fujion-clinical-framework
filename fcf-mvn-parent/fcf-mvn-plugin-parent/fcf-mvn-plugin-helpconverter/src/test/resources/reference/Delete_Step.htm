Delete a Step

  1. Expand the goal to display the steps for that goal.

  2. Highlight a step and click Review. The Edit Step pane displays.

  3. Select Deleted/Entered in Error.

Deleted/Entered in Error Option Button

  1. Select a reason for deleting the step.

  • Wrong Goal – The step is applicable for a different goal.

  • Duplicate – The step is a duplicate to another step already entered.

  • Other – Displays a field to accept a reason for deleting the step.
